Guidelines & Instructions

General Instructions

Authors from Computer Department of PCCOE are invited to submit original research papers. Submissions should align with the conference themes.


Plagiarism Warning

  • The work must be entirely original.
  • Self-plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  • Proper permissions for third-party materials must be obtained.

Format of Paper

  • File Format: PDF Only
  • Page Limit: Maximum 6 pages
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12-point
  • Margins: 1-inch all sides
  • Sections: Abstract, Intro, Methodology, Results, Conclusion
View Paper Template

Format of Presentation (PPT)

  • Slide Limit: Maximum 10 slides
  • Font: Arial or Calibri, Min 24pt
  • Time: Maximum 15 minutes per team
  • Content: Focus on results and visuals, avoid overcrowding text.
